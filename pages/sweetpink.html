The Story Behind Sweet Pink Strawberry

Sweet Pink Strawberry ice cream is a love letter to the ripe, juicy strawberries that grow in the sun-kissed fields of Sweetville. Our founder, enchanted by the vibrant pink hues at dawn, set out to bottle this beauty into a flavor. Each scoop is infused with the freshest strawberries and a touch of fairy dust, offering an enchanting twist on a timeless favorite. The Sweet Pink Strawberry soon became a symbol of playfulness and joy at IceCup, winning over hearts one pink spoonful at a time.

Toppings: Strawberry Syrup, Pink Sugar
